Meet the Author

EbMac is a Kenosha-based storyteller who believes that every soul has a name and every journey has a purpose.

After years of writing, the 283 pages of "Finding Home" became more than just a story—it became a reflection of her own path toward heritage and healing. Known to her readers as a voice for the modern woman seeking connection, she writes for those who are still navigating their way back to themselves.

"I wrote these pages so that someone else could find their way home, too."
